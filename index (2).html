Question 1 of 8
Does your headline tell people what you do and who you help?
Not your job title. A clear value proposition that answers: what problem do you solve and for whom.
Question 2 of 8
Have you posted on LinkedIn in the last 30 days?
Consistency is the foundation. An inactive profile signals an inactive professional.
Question 3 of 8
Do you receive inbound messages from people you have never reached out to?
DMs, collaboration requests, partnership conversations or opportunities coming to you without cold outreach.
Question 4 of 8
Does your About section start with a hook or with "I am a..."?
The first line of your About section decides whether anyone reads the rest of it.
Question 5 of 8
Do you have a Featured section with content, a case study or a clear call to action?
The Featured section is prime real estate. Most people leave it empty or fill it with random posts.
Question 6 of 8
When someone lands on your profile, is it immediately clear what problem you solve?
A stranger should understand your value proposition in under 5 seconds, without reading your About section.
Question 7 of 8
Have you received a speaking invite, media mention or partnership request through LinkedIn in the last 6 months?
These are signals that your profile is doing more than existing. It is actively working for you.
Question 8 of 8
Do you know which of your posts performed best and why?
If you cannot answer this, you are posting without a strategy. Content without data is guesswork.

How It Works

What actually happens
after you say yes.

No black boxes. No mystery. Here is exactly what the first month looks like, day by day.

01
Week One
We get inside your head.

Before we write a single word, we need to understand how you think, what you care about and what you want LinkedIn to do for you. This week is all about that.

Day 130-minute onboarding call. We go deep on your goals, your audience, your opinions and your industry. You talk, we listen.
Day 2Full LinkedIn profile audit delivered to you. We flag everything that is working against you and everything we will change.
Day 3Profile rewrite delivered for your approval. New headline, about section, featured section and banner recommendation.
Day 5Content strategy document shared. Your positioning angle, content pillars, post formats and a 30-day editorial calendar.
02
Week Two
First posts go live.

The profile is live, the strategy is locked and we start publishing. The first two weeks are about calibrating your voice, we write, you react, we refine.

Day 8First batch of three posts sent to you via WhatsApp or email for approval. Read, tweak if needed, approve.
Day 9First post goes live. We monitor engagement, respond to comments in your voice and flag any interesting conversations.
Day 12Second post live. We start tracking what is resonating with your specific audience and adjust accordingly.
Day 14End-of-week-two check-in. Quick WhatsApp message with early numbers and any voice adjustments from your feedback.
03
Weeks Three and Four
Consistency kicks in.

By week three, the voice is calibrated. Posts go out on schedule, engagement starts building and the profile begins doing its job, attracting the right people without you lifting a finger.

Week 3Posting continues. We also begin strategic commenting on relevant posts in your network to expand your reach organically.
Week 4Full month performance report. Impressions, profile views, follower growth, engagement rate and a qualitative summary of what moved.
Day 30Monthly strategy review call, 20 minutes. We look at what worked, what to double down on and what to shift for month two.

Month one is foundation. Month two is momentum. By month three, most clients start seeing real inbound, DMs, connection requests from the right people, speaking invitations, partnership conversations. The machine is running. Your only job is to approve posts.
